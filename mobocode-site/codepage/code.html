What's Your Sign?

Here participants can review the meanings of each of the Mobo Code symbols, find the ones that match the resources available, and learn how to put them to use in their establishments. This is also a guide for people that can use these resources.

Free Wi-Fi or open computer use available. Make sure you are courteous of other users waiting and spend your time wisely.

Food that would otherwise be thrown out at the end of the day is available. The time that it's available is shown in 24-hour format.

Public restrooms available. Please be mindful of others and respectful of this valuable resource.

Open beds or places to rest available. It’s better than a park bench.

Day-Jobs available. These jobs can be any sort of short-term, unskilled labor for a small compensation to help you make it through another day.

Drinkable water available. None of us can live without it!

Temporary shelter from weather available. These situations can include rain, hail, and snow, but it's the participant’s call to determine what constitutes extreme weather.

Clothing available. This can include second-hand clothing as well as defective or unsellable items.

Instructions for Using the Code

Download PDF (ver. 1.0)

1. Download the PDF to your computer by clicking the button above.

2. Print the PDF onto letter-size (8.5” x 11”) paper. Card stock or self-adhesive paper works best. A black & white or color printer can be used.

3. Trim the symbols out using the crop marks so that the gray border is visible within a white edge.

4. Post the symbols in a window or door so that they are visible to passers-by.
