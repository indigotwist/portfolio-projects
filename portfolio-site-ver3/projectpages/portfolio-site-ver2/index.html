I can't fix the world, but I can make it look better.

Design's purpose is not only to make things look better, but also to make things work better and to communicate the intended message effectively. I have been passionate about creating unique, visual solutions for communication problems my entire life. So far, I have specialized in print design, print production, and illustration, but I am expanding my skill-set into design and development for the web as well. Recently, I designed and coded a Three-Card Poker game using HTML, CSS, Javascript and JQuery. Learn more about who I am and what I do.
